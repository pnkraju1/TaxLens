Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 13, 2025

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Tax Lens is designed from the ground up to be a privacy-first tool. We believe you should have complete control over your financial data. Our commitment to your privacy is simple and absolute.

No Data Storage, No Servers, No Tracking

We do not store, view, or have access to your personal or financial information at any time.

  • All Processing is Client-Side: When you upload a bank statement (PDF or CSV ), all parsing, analysis, and calculations happen directly within your web browser on your own device.
  • No Data Leaves Your Computer: Your files and the information contained within them are never sent over the internet to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • No User Accounts: We do not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use Tax Lens.
  • No Cookies or Trackers: We do not use cookies or any tracking scripts to monitor your activity.

Because we never handle your data, there is nothing to store, nothing to track, and nothing to be compromised on our end. You can use Tax Lens with the full confidence that your sensitive information remains yours and yours alone.
