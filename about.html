About Tax Lens

Our Mission

In a world of complex financial transactions, it's easy to lose track of where your money is goingâ€”especially when it comes to taxes. GST, TDS, Cess, and other charges are often hidden in plain sight within lengthy bank statements. We wanted to change that.

Tax Lens was created with a simple mission: **to provide every Indian consumer, freelancer, and small business owner with a free, safe, and easy way to see the taxes they pay.** We believe that transparency leads to awareness, and awareness leads to better financial decisions.

Why We Built It

We were curious about how much tax we were paying through everyday transactions. But we couldn't find a tool that could quickly give us an answer without requiring us to upload our sensitive financial data to a third-party server. We weren't comfortable with that, and we figured others weren't either.

So, we built the tool we wanted to use: one that is powerful yet respects user privacy above all else. By processing everything directly in your browser, Tax Lens ensures that your financial information never leaves your control.
